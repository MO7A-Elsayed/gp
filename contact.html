❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best way to contact support?

For fastest response, email us at support@eduai.com. We respond within 24 hours. For urgent issues, call our toll-free number during business hours.

How do I apply to become an instructor?

Email instructors@eduai.com with your qualifications, course ideas, and teaching experience. We review applications within 5-7 business days.

Yes! We offer customized enterprise solutions. Contact partnerships@eduai.com to discuss your organization's training needs.

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee. Contact support@eduai.com with your order number and reason for the refund request.
